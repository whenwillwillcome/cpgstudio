CPG Studio
Welcome to CPG Studio, based in Brooklyn. We specialise in crafting bold strategies and creative campaigns for brands and startups that want to make noise, build community, and scale with purpose. Whether it's launching in new markets or igniting cultural relevance, we help brands find their edge and grow with authenticity.
CPG Studio is led by the founder of Minor Figures, the globally recognised cult brand that redefined the coffee industry. With deep experience in brand-building, commercial strategy, and creative storytelling, the studio helps ambitious brands scale with purpose and cultural impact. From launching in new markets to building category-defining movements, CPG Studio applies proven expertise to create brands that cut through noise and build lasting relevance.
